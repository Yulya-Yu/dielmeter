Di-El Meter

It is a device that measures dielectric permittivity of different materials. What's dielectric permittivity and how is measuring it can be useful? You will find answers to these question below!

creator of Di-El Meter

The devise was invented by Russian scientist, Valentin Yushkov, almost 30 years ago. Since that moment it was exhibited to a puplic on different conventions. The devise even was implemented on several factories throughout Russia.

areas of application

there are a lot of measuring instruments and all of them are using some physical properties as measuring parameters. However, there is one more physical property, measuring which can be useful for domestic application. This parameter is called dielectric permittivity

That is why we are proposing a new device, that can easily be used for domestic needs. This universal technology can be applied in vast variety of fields.

construction

ability to know where empty spaces and reinforcing steel beams are located in the concrete walls, their density distribution and hidden humidity.

repair works

ability to know if wooden constructions are damaged by mold or fungus.

food industry

ability to control the dynamics of cheese ripening for those who manufactures cheeses, since it can help optimize time, spent on the process.

research

can be used in medecine to find hidden abnormalities.
