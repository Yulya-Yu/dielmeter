Therefrom, Yushkov Valentin has designed the numbers of contactless, radio wave devices with one-way approach to the surface of nondestructive testing of physical and mechanical characteristics of non-metallic materials and products. Such as device Emiksi-1 (electromagnetic quality indicator for construction products, which is intended to measure the dielectric permittivity and detect dissimilarities in non-metallic materials). With the help of this device the correlation of the concrete strength and its dielectric permittivity were discovered for the first time.

ISBK-1 DEVICE

Contactless device, that measures electrical resistivity of conductive metallic and semi-conductive coatings of dielectric materials. is plotted on the moving Mylar in the evacuated vessel.

CONTOUR 1 DEVICE

Device which is intended to measure the dielectric permittivity - implemented copy of Emiksi-1.

ZOND 1 DEVICE

Digital modification of ICBK 1.

additional information

usage

Device Emiksi-1 can be used to control the distribution of solidity of concrete in the concrete products and location of reinforcing steel and hollows in these products. It also can be used to control the roadway covering in motion. The results of developing of monitoring method, control means and its applications were published in 19 printed articles. The Emiksi and Zond 1 devices were exhibited at various exhibitions.

structure of the devise

Structurally, the devices consist of a compact measuring unit and remote sensor (transducer). One of the modifications of Emiksi was tested in order to observe the dynamics of changes in the electrophysical properties of hardening concrete mix, which was prepared in a steam-heat warming mode. For this purpose, the sensor was installed in the slot of one of the sections of the cartridge unit, which was designed to manufacture concrete products in steam treatment mode. The working surface of the sensor was separated from the concrete mix with radio parent partition.

current state

Currently, it is impossible to bring these results into industrial application due to the lack of funding. Given the funding, it is expected to expand the informational content of the characteristics, which are measured by the control devices. Moreover, it is expected to replace the components and materials, which are used in the existing prototypes of the control devices, to the modern ones. It is also necessary to develop the technology of the device production and to create new prototypes of the control devices.
